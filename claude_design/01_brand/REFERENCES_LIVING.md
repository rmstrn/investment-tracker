## Derived patterns (populated 2026-04-24 Stage 0)

See `VOICE_PROFILE.md` for the full operational derivation. Quick summary below for reference.

### Positive fingerprint

- **Syllable distribution:** 33% 1-syl · 58% 2-syl · 8% 2-3-syl · 0% 3+ syl
- **Letter count range:** 3–7, median 6, mean 5.5 (tight band around short-compact)
- **Onset class dominant:** hard-edged — plosive 33% + fricative 25% + plosive-cluster (within plosive) → **58% hard-edged**; liquid 25%; nasal 8%; vowel 8%
- **Close class dominant:** bimodal — plosive-close 33% + liquid-close (-r / -l tail) 33%; nasal 17%; open-vowel 17%
- **Semantic strategy distribution:** real-word-shadow 33% + real-word-unchanged 25% + concept-word 8% = **67% real-word territory**; coined 17%; proper-name 8%; compound 8%; mythology 0%
- **Archetype primary:** Magician 67% (dominant)
- **Archetype modifier:** Sage 50% + Everyman 42% (blended, both alive)
- **Tone register:** modern tech-tool 2020s · consumer-facing · confident-but-warm · «invented-but-rooted» over «invented-from-scratch»

### Negative anti-targets

- **Phonetic shapes to avoid:** 3+ syllable academic cadence (Noesis); soft-nasal-open-vowel cascade in non-English form (Shunkan, Teven); consonant-cluster pure-slug (Lerx); single-syllable mystical-root (Bodh); **compound English-word stacks ≥8 letters (Stillwatch, Slowturn shape — drift «descriptive tool» not «discovered name»)**
- **Semantic territories to avoid:** Greek/Latin academic abstraction; Sanskrit/Pali mystical roots; companion-warm human-first-name territory; meaningless-invented-slug; exotic-for-flavor non-English; **negative-valence English head morpheme (slow-, dim-, dull-, late-, lag-)**; **mythology-only-because-domain-is-clean (semantic-fit-к-product fails the «one-sentence test»)**
- **Wrong archetype flags:** Sidekick (Teven), Mystic/Sage-only-overbalanced (Noesis, Bodh), Outlaw-brutal (implied by PO's Notion-restrained lock)
- **Tone registers to reject:** academic-philosophical, spiritual-meditative, warm-companion-sidekick, tech-startup-generator, exotic-phonetic-for-flavor, **fintech-infrastructure-serious-only**, **descriptive-observation-tool (compound English «watch / form / mark / span» tail)**
