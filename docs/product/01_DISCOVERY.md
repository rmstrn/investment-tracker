# 01 — Product Discovery (Market Research 2026-04-22)

## Key finding

Market has moved since `00_PROJECT_BRIEF.md` was written. The original positioning — "AI-native трекер, разговор вместо таблиц" — is **validated but no longer uniquely occupied**. Multiple 2024-2026 challengers claim "AI + investing" territory, each from different angles.

## Competitive landscape 2026

### Trading-chat (executes trades via chat)
- **Magnifi** — AI investing copilot, aggregates brokerages, answers in plain English + trades ETFs/stocks/funds
- **Bobby (RockFlow)** — full-stack AI trade agent + quant portfolio deployment, invite-only
- **Public.com AI Agents** — chat + trading platform

### Research-chat (market/company Q&A, no portfolio context)
- **Fiscal.ai** — ChatGPT-like research platform with institutional-grade data
- **WarrenAI** (Investing.com, April 2025) — Buffett-style Q&A
- **Gainify** — AI tools for investing
- **AI Allie (Alinea Invest)** — SEC-regulated micro-investing chatbot

### HNW wealth management (advisor replacement)
- **Range** — $12M Series A (Google AI fund), CFPs + AI, replaces financial advisor
- **Arta Finance** — AI agents for wealth, $100K+ AUM free, $20/mo starter tier
- **Parthean** — B2B AI for financial advisors (not consumer)

### Dashboard-legacy (tracker, minimal or no AI)
- **Snowball Analytics** — dividend-focused, $79.99/year, 1000+ brokers via Yodlee/SnapTrade, **NO AI**
- **Kubera** — $150/yr net worth tracker, design-forward, added "AI Advisor" but supporting role
- **Empower** (formerly Personal Capital) — free dashboard, upsell human advisor
- **Sharesight** — position tracking, tax-focused, AU-origin
- **Getquin**, **Finary**, **Delta** — EU dashboard-first

## Uncontested wedge

**"AI chat about YOUR portfolio, read-only, for retail, no trading, no advisor upsell"** — not fully occupied.

Differentiation map:
- Magnifi/Bobby/Public: chat-first but **push trades** (we don't)
- Range/Arta: AI-first but **HNW-only** ($100K+)
- WarrenAI/Fiscal.ai: chat but **no portfolio context**
- Snowball/Kubera: **no AI as primary UX**
- Empower: free + advisor upsell, not chat

## ICP (best-fit customer)

### A — Multi-broker millennial (primary for Plus tier)
- Age: 28-40
- Geo: US primary, UK, EU
- Portfolio: $20K-100K
- Brokers: 2-3 (Fidelity 401k + Schwab/Robinhood taxable + Coinbase)
- Asset classes: stocks, ETFs, some crypto
- Income: $75-200K
- Tech: iPhone, weekly ChatGPT user
- Behavior: tried spreadsheet tracker, abandoned

### B — AI-native newcomer (primary for Free → Plus conversion)
- Age: 22-32
- Geo: US primary
- Portfolio: $2K-20K
- Brokers: 1-2
- Asset classes: stocks/ETFs + some crypto
- Income: $40-100K
- Tech: mobile-first, daily ChatGPT, TikTok/Reels
- Behavior: posts about investing progress, reads r/personalfinance

### NOT our customer
- HNW $500K+ → Range/Arta territory
- Active day traders → want execution, not insights
- Dividend-only power users → prefer Snowball
- Pure crypto natives → CoinTracker, Kubera
- Human advisor seekers → need CFP

## Trends tailwinding positioning

1. LLM-first consumer apps (ChatGPT mainstream since 2023)
2. Multi-broker / multi-currency retail reality
3. Post-2022 advisor distrust (volatility + fee scrutiny)
4. Open-finance normalization (SnapTrade, Plaid)
5. Post-ChatGPT financial literacy curiosity

## Acquisition channels (derived from ICP)

- **Reddit** r/personalfinance, r/investing (A)
- **TikTok / Reels** — finance creators (B)
- **YouTube** — finance creators (both A & B)
- **Twitter/X** — retail investor community
- NOT LinkedIn wealth advisor tier, NOT financial conferences — those are Range/Arta channels
