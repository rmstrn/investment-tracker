## 2. Brand world / manifesto

**Provedo is an AI guide that walks you through your portfolio — never ahead of you, never behind, always alongside.**

The name carries dual native meaning that no other candidate across 32 generation rounds offered. In Italian, *provedere* is a 13th-century verb meaning «to provide for, to foresee, to take care of» — the act of looking ahead on someone's behalf without taking the decision for them. In Russian, the same phonetic shape «прове́до» reads natively as «проведу» — «I will lead through». Italian listeners hear «I take care of your future»; Russian listeners hear «I will guide you through». The product does both: it sees patterns across your full portfolio that you would miss across separate broker apps, and it explains those patterns in plain language so you stay in charge of the decision. That is the Magician (pattern synthesis from real data with sources) + Sage (calm observation, foresight, no preaching) + Everyman (accessible, no jargon-as-gatekeeping, friendly without being cute) blend in one name — the archetype trio attached to a real verb, not a coined slug.

**The JTBD Provedo specifically owns:** «notice patterns I'd miss across all my brokers + explain them to me in plain language, without telling me what to do». That is a Sage claim (observation + clarity), a Magician delivery (multi-broker synthesis the user couldn't run themselves), and an Everyman promise (anti-paternalism — Provedo does not buy or sell on your behalf, does not nudge you toward outcomes, does not optimize you toward someone else's revenue). For multi-broker millennials (28-40, $20K-100K, productivity-native — the Notion/Obsidian cohort) and AI-native newcomers (22-32, $2-20K) who already live in chat-first interfaces, Provedo is the first portfolio tool that matches the cognitive register they bring to the rest of their work. Free is always Free — the brand commits that the core value (multi-broker aggregation + chat + weekly insights) never moves behind a paywall, because the moment a portfolio brain becomes a sales surface, it stops being trustworthy.

2. **#2 «Provedo проведёт через твой портфель»** — keep as **RU-market exclusive tagline** (alongside #1 as primary). 5. **#5 «Foresight on what you actually own»** — pulls etymology forward but adds regulatory borderline. Hold as alternate; not recommended primary.

## 4. Brand voice fingerprint

This section summarizes — does NOT duplicate — `BRAND_VOICE/VOICE_PROFILE.md`. Source-of-truth for tone rubric scoring is the master VOICE_PROFILE.md document (7-criterion C1-C7, Muni-shape sub-anchor, full anti-target list). Brand-foundation summary follows:

**Three-archetype blend (per VOICE_PROFILE.md derivation 67%/50%/42% match to positioning v3.1):**

- **Magician (primary, 67% reference signal).** Provedo delivers «wow moment» pattern synthesis tied to real data with sources. The Italian verb-form root «I provide for / I foresee» is itself the Magician signal — coined-but-rooted in real Romance morphology, not invention from scratch. The RU bilingual decode «I will lead through» is Magician delivery: the user gets two layers of meaning for the price of one sound.
- **Sage (strong modifier, 50% reference signal).** Provedo notices, explains, observes — never preaches. Calm, specific, intellectual without snobbery. The Notion/Obsidian/Linear/Cursor register applied to finance. The product surfaces patterns; the user makes the call.
- **Everyman (warmth modifier, 42% reference signal).** Provedo is accessible to retail, not corporate-cold. No jargon-as-gatekeeping. Inline vocabulary unpacking when needed. «Provedo заметил…» reads like a knowledgeable friend, not a robo-advisor.

**Anti-archetype registers explicitly rejected:** Sidekick (pet-companion warmth — Teven shape), Outlaw-brutal (edgy/disruptive-finance), Sage-overdosed-academic (Noesis lecture-hall), Mystic-pure (Bodh spiritual register).

## 6. Brand-name usage rules

These rules govern every appearance of «Provedo» in product copy, marketing, microcopy, AI system prompts, and external communication. They are mandatory — not stylistic preferences.

### 6.1 Capitalization + spelling

- **Always «Provedo»** (capital P, lowercase remainder).
- **Never PROVEDO** (all-caps; reads as shouting / shareholder-letter brand).
- **Never provedo** (lowercase; reads as URL fragment, not brand-mark).
- **Latin script primary** in EN and RU contexts both. Cyrillic «Прове́до» appears ONLY in pronunciation guides with explicit stress mark, never in body copy.
- **No alternate spellings** — never «Provido», «Proveda», «Provedos».

### 6.2 Verb-form rules (critical for 5-item EN guardrails)

**EN verb allowlist** (Provedo can do these things):
- provides clarity
- provides context
- provides observation
- provides foresight
- notices
- explains
- surfaces
- reads patterns
- holds context
- maps holdings

**EN verb BANNED list** (Provedo never does these things — Lane A regulatory + brand-voice):
- ❌ provides advice
- ❌ provides recommendations
- ❌ provides strategy
- ❌ provides suggestions
- ❌ advises
- ❌ recommends
- ❌ suggests
- ❌ tells you to (buy / sell / rebalance / hold)
- ❌ guides your decision (use «walks you through» instead — different register)

**«Guidance» splitter rule:** prefer «Provedo provides clarity» over «Provedo provides guidance». «Guidance» sits FCA/FINRA-borderline (regulator readers parse it as advice-adjacent); «clarity» does not. Source: `docs/legal/lane-a-language-rules.md` (when content-lead consolidates) — interim rule lives here.

**RU verb-form rules:**
- RU: «Provedo проведёт / проводит / провёл» (natural — verb-of-leading-through, not verb-of-advising)
- RU: «Provedo заметил / замечает / показывает / объясняет»
- RU BANNED: «Provedo советует / рекомендует / предлагает [покупать/продавать]»

### 6.3 «Ask Provedo» pattern

The locked hero pattern uses imperative-to-product:
- EN: «Ask your portfolio» (hero) / «Ask Provedo about your portfolio» (microcopy variant)
- RU: «Спроси свой портфель» (hero) / «Спроси Provedo о своём портфеле» (microcopy variant)

Avoid forced verb-of-Provedo construction in EN: ❌ «Provedo it» reads unnatural (unlike «Slack him» or «Notion it» which fit native verb adoption). The product does not need to verb in EN — the imperative «Ask Provedo» carries the action.

### 6.4 Persona declaration pattern

When Provedo speaks in first-person (Coach pushes, AI responses, onboarding):
- EN: «I'm Provedo. I notice patterns across your brokers and explain them in plain language.»
- RU: «Я Provedo. Я замечаю паттерны в твоих брокерах и объясняю их простыми словами.»

Persona-fit C6 score = 6-7 (per VOICE_PROFILE.md rubric — slight clinical lean, but acceptable for Sage-Magician-Everyman blend, not a Sidekick warmth).

### 6.5 Co-occurrence audit list (5-item EN guardrail per `03_NAMING.md` action item #6)

In any EN copy where «Provedo» appears, the following words **must NOT appear in the same sentence or adjacent clause**:

1. **advice / advises / advisory** — banned co-occurrence (Lane A line)
2. **recommend / recommendation** — banned co-occurrence
3. **strategy / strategic** — banned co-occurrence (FINRA-flag)
4. **suggest / suggestion** — banned co-occurrence
5. **tell you / tells you** (in context of action) — banned co-occurrence

**AI EN system-prompt guardrail** (for chat surface, must be in every model invocation):
> «You are Provedo. You provide clarity, observation, foresight, and context — never advice, recommendations, strategy, or suggestions. When asked to recommend an action, redirect to observation: 'I can show you the pattern I see in your holdings; the decision is yours.'»

**Behavioral-coach EN copy especially audit.** Coach pushes are the highest-risk surface for inadvertent advice-language drift. Every coach-push template must be content-lead reviewed against this guardrail before shipping.
