## 5. Implications for our hero

**Our current hero:** "Поговори со своим портфелем. Просто задай вопрос."

Against this audit:
- **Strong:** Chat-first framing is less crowded in English hero-space than expected. Only Origin has close variant ("Track everything. Ask anything.") — and "ask anything" applies to tracking + investing + spending, not specifically to portfolio. Our "Поговори со своим портфелем" is MORE portfolio-specific.
- **Strong:** Russian-language is uncontested — zero overlap.
- **Risk:** English translation ("Talk to your portfolio." / "Just ask.") would sit slightly adjacent to Origin. Not a killer but requires differentiation below the fold.
- **Strong:** We avoid the "AI" adjective in hero — which is refreshingly different from the AI-flavored crowd (Magnifi, Moomoo, Tickeron, Origin, PortfolioPilot, etc.)

**Recommendation:** Hold current hero. Ensure below-fold cleanly contrasts us from Origin on: (a) investing-pure not budgeting; (b) not-advisor framing; (c) multi-broker aggregation on par with PortfolioPilot claim.
