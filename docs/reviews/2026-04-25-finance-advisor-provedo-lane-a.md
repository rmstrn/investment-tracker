## Phase 1 — Investor psychology axis

### Etymological promise

Provedo = Italian *provedere / provvedere* (1st-person-singular form by analogy with Memoro's grammatical pattern) ← Latin *providere* = «to see ahead / foresee / make provision for». Modern Italian semantic field: «I provide for / I take care of / I anticipate / I arrange». Romance-language readers parse it as «I provide / I see ahead».

Russian back-mapping: «прове́до» phonetically aligns with «проведу» = future 1st person of «провести» = «I will lead through / I will conduct / I will guide». Clean sherpa-metaphor reading in RU.

This is a **capability + caring root**, not a direct outcome-claim like Lucri. It encodes *what the product does for you* (provides, foresees, leads through) — not *the result you get* (profit). That is the structural difference from Lucri, and it matters.

### Target-user attraction profile

Pattern continued from Lucri table:

| Brand | Etymological promise | Audience pull |
|---|---|---|
| Memoro (locked) | I remember (capability/neutral) | reflective long-term holder ✓ ICP A |
| Stillwatch | observe (capability) | reflective long-term holder ✓ ICP A |
| Lucri | I profit (outcome) | outcome-seeking, leans active-trader ✗ anti-ICP |
| **Provedo** | I provide for / lead through (capability + care) | **caring-guidance seeker — split signal** |

Provedo's etymological signal pulls toward users who want **someone to take care of their financial life** — which is a real ICP-adjacent cohort but creates a specific Lane A tension below.

- **ICP A (Notion/Obsidian-native multi-broker millennial, long-term reflective):** «Provedo» reads as Sage-companion («provides clarity, foresees what you'd miss»). Aligned with Memoro target. ✓
- **ICP B (AI-native newcomer, $2-20K):** «Provide» registers as caring-helper — neutral-positive, doesn't recruit anti-ICP. Mild concern: newcomer cohort may read «provide» as «provide advice / provide recommendations», pre-loading wrong mental model. Mitigatable via copy, not structural. ⚠️
- **Anti-ICP (day-trader / get-rich-quick):** Provedo does NOT recruit this cohort the way Lucri does. «Provide for retirement / family» is long-term framing, structurally opposite to day-trade. ✓

Net: Provedo's audience pull is structurally cleaner than Lucri's. The concern is not anti-ICP recruitment; it's **role ambiguity** (Sage-guide vs Advisor).

### The Sage / Advisor splitter

Lane A locked positioning depends on a precise distinction:

- «I provide guidance / clarity / observation» = Sage = Lane A clean ✓
- «I provide advice / recommendations / a strategy» = Advisor = Lane A FAIL ✗

The verb «provide» lives exactly at this splitter. Provedo's name is intelligible in both directions; brand-voice + AI tone discipline must consistently anchor it on the Sage side. This is real but mitigable.

«Provide for» semantically sits in the same family as «take care of / look after» — caring, not extracting. Direct opposite of Lucri's «filthy lucre» shadow. The brand is named after the *thing the company commits to be* (caring guide), not the thing it commits not to be.

Provedo carries **none of the structural Free-is-Free tension** that Lucri carried. The «provide» semantic is consistent with anti-mercenary commitment.

Subtle concern: «provider» in US insurance/healthcare context has acquired commercial-vendor connotations («network provider»). For ICP A US users, this may register as mildly transactional. Not a blocker; below detection threshold for most readers.

## Phase 4 — AI content downstream tone risk (EN/RU asymmetry — the critical finding)

The product is AI-native; brand name is the AI persona's anchor (R26 logic). When anchor = «provide», the AI tone calibration drifts toward provision-framed output. Asymmetry between EN and RU markets:

### English-side concrete examples

| AI output | Lane A status |
|---|---|
| «Provedo provides clarity on your dividend stream» | clean (clarity = observation) ✓ |
| «Provedo provides an analysis of your tech allocation» | clean (analysis = observation) ✓ |
| «Provedo provides a recommendation on rebalancing» | **DRIFT — recommendation = advice** ✗ |
| «Provedo provides guidance on tax-loss harvesting» | **borderline — «guidance» is the splitter word** ⚠️ |
| «Provedo provides a personalized investment strategy for you» | **TRIGGERS Lane A regulatory** ✗✗ |
| «Your Provedo provider says...» | unfortunate insurance-coded register ⚠️ |
| Weekly insight push: «3 things Provedo foresaw this week» | clean (foresaw = observation) ✓ |

The risk is **gradual drift in AI tone calibration** — the verb «provide» creates a downhill semantic gradient in English toward «provide advice / recommendation / strategy», three words that map directly to RIA-regulated activity. Six months of unattended copy iteration and the AI persona will subconsciously align with the «provider of advice» mental model.

This is the **same mechanism** as Lucri's «profit-spotting» drift — but the gradient is **milder**. «Provide» does not directly equal «advise»; the drift requires one extra step (provide → provide advice). Lucri's drift required zero steps (the name itself is the outcome). Quantitatively: lighter tax than Lucri, heavier tax than Memoro.

### Russian-side: clean

«Прове́до» / «проведу» in Russian = «I will lead through / I will conduct». This maps to sherpa metaphor — guide-companion, not advisor. The verb «провести» does not have an «advice» splitter pair in the same way English «provide» does. RU copy needs no special guardrails.

### Asymmetry implication

EN copy needs **more guardrails than RU copy** for the same brand. This is unusual — most bilingual product names have symmetric tone risk. Provedo's risk profile is asymmetric because Italian etymology («provide for») drifts toward advice-language only in English; Russian phonetic-association («проведу = lead through») does not.

Operational consequence: content-lead's EN style guide for Provedo needs explicit anti-drift rules. RU style guide doesn't. Brand-voice-curator should be briefed on this asymmetry.

## Phase 5 — Verdict: WARN

**Honest assessment:** Provedo is **not a clean SUPPORT** but is materially cleaner than Lucri. Three findings:

1. **Audience pull:** Provedo does not recruit anti-ICP day-trader cohort the way Lucri did. Pull is toward caring-guidance seekers — adjacent to ICP A reflective-long-term-holder, not opposed. ✓
2. **Free-is-Free coherence:** HIGH. «Provide for / lead through» is anti-mercenary semantic, consistent with locked commitment. No compensating disclaimers needed at brand-strategy level. ✓
3. **EN-side advice-drift:** REAL. The verb «provide» creates a downhill gradient toward «provide advice / recommendation / strategy». Mitigatable with copy guardrails (3-4 items, lighter than Lucri's 6-item tax). ⚠️

**Provedo is also not a clean REJECT.** The structural problems that broke Lucri (outcome-root etymology, anti-ICP audience pull, Free-is-Free tension) are absent. The remaining risk (EN-side advice-drift) is operationally containable with disciplined copy rules.

**Therefore: WARN.** Provedo can ship Lane A safely with a 3-4 item copy-guardrail compensating discipline. Memoro requires zero such discipline — but if PO has chosen Provedo for non-finance reasons (brand-strategist R32 ranked it 8.7), the finance-domain cost is acceptable.

Comparative tax burden:
- Memoro: 0 items
- Provedo: 3-4 items (EN-side only)
- Lucri: 6 items (rejected baseline)

## Pre-launch tone-discipline requirements (if WARN confirmed)

If Provedo is locked, finance-advisor recommends to brand-voice-curator + content-lead:

1. **Banned EN co-occurrence rule:** the word «Provedo» must never appear in same sentence as: «advice / advise / recommendation / recommend / strategy / strategic / suggestion / suggest». Hard rule for content-lead EN style guide. RU style guide unaffected.
2. **AI persona-prompt guardrail (EN system prompt):** explicit instruction — «You are Provedo. The name comes from Italian/Latin *providere* = to foresee + Russian *проведу* = to lead through. You provide *clarity, observation, foresight, context* — never *advice, recommendations, or strategy*. Frame all outputs as observation, not prescription.»
3. **Verbs-allowlist for Provedo-EN copy:** «provides clarity / provides context / provides observation / sees ahead / foresees / leads through / surfaces / notices». Banned: «provides advice / provides recommendations / provides a strategy / provides guidance on what to do».
4. **«Guidance» splitter rule:** the word «guidance» is borderline in financial regulatory context (FCA «guidance» specifically, FINRA Rule 2210 «recommendation» framework). Recommend content-lead avoid «Provedo provides guidance» entirely; use «Provedo provides clarity» or «Provedo provides observation» as Lane A-clean alternatives.
5. **Behavioral-coach copy especially audited (EN):** the coach surface is the highest Lane A risk surface. Provedo + coach + EN = stack three risk vectors. Every coach observation must be observation-only («Provedo noticed you sold AAPL three times at the local low») not provision-of-advice («Provedo suggests reviewing your sell triggers»).

RU copy: no special guardrails needed. «Проведу через» / «проведёт сквозь» / «помогает увидеть» — all clean.

This is a real operational tax — but materially smaller than Lucri's. Three items in EN style guide + one AI system-prompt clause + one surface-specific audit rule = manageable.
